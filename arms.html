Arm Exercises

Bicep Curls

Description: A classic isolation exercise to grow and define the biceps.

How to do: Hold dumbbells at your sides, curl them up while keeping elbows close to your torso.

Tricep Dips

Description: A bodyweight exercise focusing on the triceps.

How to do: Use a bench or parallel bars, lower your body and press back up through your arms.

Hammer Curls

Description: Works the biceps and forearms with a neutral grip.

How to do: Hold dumbbells with palms facing each other and curl them upward.

Tricep Pushdowns

Description: Cable-based movement to isolate and define the triceps.

How to do: Push the cable bar down until arms are straight, then return slowly.

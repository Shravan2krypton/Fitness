Build a Powerful Back

Back Exercises

Deadlifts

Description: One of the most effective compound movements for overall back strength.

How to do: Stand behind the barbell, hinge at the hips, grip the bar, and lift by driving through your legs and back.

Pull-Ups

Description: A bodyweight staple targeting the lats and upper back.

How to do: Hang from a bar with palms facing away and pull your chin above the bar.

Barbell Rows

Description: Strengthens the mid-back and rear delts.

How to do: Bend at the hips with a barbell, row toward your abdomen, and squeeze your shoulder blades.

Lat Pulldown

Description: Targets the lats for width and definition.

How to do: Pull the bar down in front of your chest while seated and control it back up slowly.

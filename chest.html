Build a Stronger Chest

Chest Exercises

Push-Ups

Description: A bodyweight exercise that targets the entire chest and triceps.

How to do: Keep your back straight, lower yourself until your chest almost touches the ground, then push back up.

Bench Press

Description: A compound movement using a barbell to develop upper body strength.

How to do: Lie on a bench, grip the barbell shoulder-width apart, lower to your chest, and press upward.

Incline Dumbbell Press

Description: Focuses on the upper chest and shoulders using dumbbells.

How to do: Sit on an incline bench, press the dumbbells upward, then lower slowly.

Chest Fly

Description: Isolates chest muscles using dumbbells or a machine.

How to do: With a slight bend in elbows, open arms wide and bring them back together like hugging a barrel.

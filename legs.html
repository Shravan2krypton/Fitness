Train Your Foundation

Leg Exercises

Squats

Description: A foundational lower-body movement that targets quads, glutes, and hamstrings.

How to do: Stand shoulder-width apart, lower hips until thighs are parallel to the ground, and push through heels to return.

Leg Press

Description: A machine-based compound movement that works the legs in a controlled motion.

How to do: Push the platform away with your feet, keeping knees slightly bent at the top.

Lunges

Description: Great for isolating each leg and improving balance and symmetry.

How to do: Step forward, lower your back knee toward the ground, then push back to standing position.

Leg Curls

Description: Isolates the hamstrings using a leg curl machine.

How to do: Sit or lie on the machine and curl your heels toward your glutes with control.

Build Strong, Defined Shoulders

Shoulder Exercises

Overhead Press

Description: Targets all three heads of the shoulder muscle using a barbell or dumbbells.

How to do: Press the weights overhead while keeping your core engaged and back straight.

Lateral Raises

Description: Focuses on the side deltoid to create width.

How to do: Raise dumbbells to the side until shoulder height, keeping elbows slightly bent.

Front Raises

Description: Targets the front deltoid for definition and size.

How to do: Lift dumbbells forward to shoulder height with arms straight.

Reverse Pec Deck

Description: Isolates the rear deltoids using a machine or cables.

How to do: Sit facing the machine, keep arms extended, and pull back using rear shoulder muscles.
